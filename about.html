About

Momentum Shift is a two player cooperative puzzle game that makes unique use of the split screen. In fact, a core mechanic of Momentum Shift requires players to swap locations with their above/below screen partner. In doing so, previous physics such as speed will still remain the same even after a swap has occured. This swapping occurence is an extremely interesting phenomenon for the humans of Momentum Shift. Earth has become uninhabitable after their extensive use of fuels, and the only safehaven remains in secluded biodomes with limited resources. Unable to effectively travel from one dome to another, humans are desparate for an alternative method of transportation.

Enter Applesoft, a company that has bioengineered a gene capable of swapping two organisms regardless of distance. Before annoucing anything to the public, the swapping gene now dubbed as SHIFT must go through extensive testing phases. Thus, Applesoft has integrated SHIFT into the genomes of a pair of chicken siblings. While the birds were initially eager to solve puzzles, they eventually grew bored and refused to utilize SHIFT according to the researcher's demands. Angry and frustrated, Applesoft researchers separated the fowl from one another, forcing them to solve complicated tests in order to reunite.
